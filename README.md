## Welcome to Ori!

Ori is your go-to online messaging platform for real-time communication, currently under active development. It's powered by Node.js and Express on the backend, and HTML, SCSS, and JavaScript on the frontend. With Firebase handling authentication and the database, and real-time chat using Socket.io and WebSockets, Ori is set to provide a seamless messaging experience.

## Key Features

- **Real-Time Messaging:** Instantly exchange messages with friends and groups in real-time.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Ori's intuitive design ensures a seamless messaging experience.
- **Secure and Private:** Your privacy and security are our top priorities. We use state-of-the-art encryption to protect your conversations.
- **Customizable:** Personalize your profile, chat settings, and notifications to suit your preferences.
- **Group Chats:** Create or join group chats to stay connected with multiple friends or colleagues at once.
- **File Sharing:** Share documents, images, videos, and more with ease.
- **Emojis and Stickers:** Express yourself with a wide range of emojis and stickers.
- **Cross-Platform:** Ori is planned to be available on web, mobile, and desktop, making it easy to stay connected from anywhere.

## Technology Stack

Ori is being built using a modern and powerful technology stack:

- **Backend:**

  - SCSS
  - JavaScript

- **Authentication and Database:**
  - Firebase

- **Real-Time Chat:**
  - Socket.io
